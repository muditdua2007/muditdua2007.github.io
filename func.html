About ChatGPT :

ChatGPT is a chatbot developed by OpenAI that uses a large language model to generate human-like text based on prompts from users. It enables users to refine and steer a conversation towards a desired length, format, style, level of detail, and language. Successive user prompts and replies are considered at each conversation stage as context.
